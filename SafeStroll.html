Getting students home safe.

Do you ever find it uncomfortable to walk around campus at night?
Do you ever wish you had someone to walk home with?
Have you wished that there was an alternative to Tufts SafeRide, where university police drive students home?
Would you want to ensure that your peers feel safe?

SafeStroll is a non-profit organization aiming to ensure that students on college campuses get home safe. The program we offer involves a group of student volunteers as well as a mobile app that matches pairs of volunteers to a student looking for someone to walk with. One of our goals is to promote a safe community on campus, and SafeStroll facilitates the development of trust among the student body by uniting users and volunteers.
